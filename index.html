    • KNOW YOUR STRAND
      ABM

      ABM strand focuses on the basic concepts of Financial management, Business and Management, Corporate operations, and all things that are accounted for.

      Job Opportunities
      Accountant, Economist, Financial Analyst, Entrepreneur, Marketer, Sales Manager, Banker, Auditor, Investor, Stockbroker, ETC.
      HUMSS

      HUMSS strand is designed for those who wonder what is on the other side of the wall. In other words, you are ready to take on the world and talk a lot of people.

      Job Opportunities
      Teacher, Lawyer, Psychologist, Author, Politician, Criminologist, Journalist, News Anchor, Sociologist, Historian, ETC.
      GAS

      GAS is a Senior High School strand that takes on a generalist approach in prepairing students for college. It covers a various discipline like Humanities, Social Sciences, Organization, and Management.

      Job Opportunities
      Since GAS does not specialize in any strand, you are free to choose any college degree program under the three other strands based on the elective you will choose. Moreover, a possible course to pursue from this strand is Education.
      ICT-Programming

      Programming is a highly creative and dynamic field that requires problem-solving skills, logical thinking, attention to detail, and continious learning.

      Job Opportunities
      Software Developer / Engineer, Web Developer, Data Scientist, Cybersecurity Analyst, Automation Engineer.
      ICT-Animation

      Animation can serve a various purposes, including entertainment, education, communication, and story telling.

      Job Opportunities
      Digital Illustrator, Motion Graphic Designer, 2D Animator, Computer Graphics Designer
      ICT-CSS

      CSS Teaches students how to install, configure, and maintain computer systems and networks. It develops students' skills in diagnosis and troubleshooting of computer systems, as well as in the performance of computer operations.

      Job Opportunities
      Software / Hardware Engineer, Network Engineer, Computer Technician, Technical Support Officer, Computer Hardware Specialist.
